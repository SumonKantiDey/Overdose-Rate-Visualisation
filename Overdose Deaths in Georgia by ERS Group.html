Overdose Deaths per 100,000 in Georgia

Drug overdose death rate per 100,000 residents, aggregated by county Economic Risk Score (ERS) group across three reporting periods. Normalizing by population removes the confound between county size and economic risk.
ERS 1— Lowest economic risk  (26 counties)
ERS 2— Moderate economic risk  (32 counties)
ERS 3— Elevated economic risk  (39 counties)
ERS 4— Highest economic risk  (44 counties)  † partial data
Source: Georgia drug overdose death data by county, 2010–2021. Rates computed over non-suppressed counties only.
† ERS 4 rates cover only the subset of counties with non-suppressed death counts (19–25 of 44 counties per period); interpret with caution.
ERS 5 omitted — all 18 counties fully suppressed across all periods.
Note: y-axis begins at 30, not 0. Differences between groups are visually amplified.
